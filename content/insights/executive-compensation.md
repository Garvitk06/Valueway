# Designing Fintech Compensation

Sourcing C-suite leadership in Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sectors has become highly competitive. Traditional cash packages are no longer enough to attract top-tier innovators. Modern plans require balanced equity, performance bonuses, and custom vesting structures.

## Benchmarking Cash vs. Equity

The split between basic salary and variable compensation depends on the scale of the firm:

*   **Early Stage Series-A/B**: Lower cash ($140k - $180k), higher equity (1.5% - 3.0%) with performance milestones.
*   **Late Stage Growth / Unicorns**: Market-rate cash ($220k - $300k), option pools with performance-vesting schedules.
*   **Established BFSI Corporates**: High basic cash ($350k+), significant cash performance bonuses, and restricted stock units (RSUs).

## Implementing Performance Vesting

Performance-based vesting bridges the gap between shareholder return and executive motivation. Milestones commonly evaluate:

*   Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) growth targets
*   Successful regulatory audits and compliance clearances
*   Launch schedules for core blockchain or fintech services

## Long-Term Retention Tools

Vesting periods should extend beyond the standard 4-year cycle. Using double-trigger vesting, performance-accelerated options, and structured bonus retention schedules ensures alignment over a 5 to 7 year horizon.
